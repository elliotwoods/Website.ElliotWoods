About

Elliot Woods is a digital media artist from Manchester. He tests possible futures between humans and visual design technologies (e.g. cameras, projectors, computation). He is known for being the first to 3D calibrate a Kinect to a video projector, for creating monumental scale light field projections, for drawing a volumetric image of the sun out of sunlight, and for releasing approximately 200 open source creative tools and libraries. Elliot co-founded Kimchi and Chips, an experimental art studio based in Seoul with Mimi Son which creates public artworks which uncover new technical and aesthetic territory. He applies his past studies in physics to produce sense-able phenomena from abstract domains.
